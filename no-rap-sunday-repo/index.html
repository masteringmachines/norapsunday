How the Contraption Works

BY OUR TECHNOLOGY CORRESPONDENT

Witnesses report that the machine accepts common audio files by the simple act of dropping them upon its viewing glass. The record is read entirely within the visitor's own browser — our engineers confirm not a single byte departs the premises.

Once fed, the orb — rendered in fine wireframe and stippled in the halftone manner of this very publication — deforms bodily to the bass, quickens its rings to the middle registers, and scatters ink to the treble. Gentlemen and ladies may seize the orb and turn it by hand.

Notices

THE WAVEFORM. Beneath the glass, the record's true shape is printed bar by bar. Tap any point to travel there at once.

THE LEDGER. Shuffle and repeat may be armed at the buttons flanking the transport. The house keeps count.

THE VAULT. Every record fed to the machine is catalogued below in the classifieds, engraved with its own pressing number.
